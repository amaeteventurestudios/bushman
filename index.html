What's Inside

Every Part. Every Tool. Zero Guesswork.

01
Pre-Cut Chassis Panels

Aircraft-grade aluminium panels, laser-cut and deburred, ready to bolt together. Lightweight yet sturdy enough for off-road terrain.

02
Dual Motor System

Two high-torque brushless motors with independent drive control. Tackle slopes, sand, and uneven ground without breaking a sweat.

03
Open-Source Control Board

Arduino-compatible main board with wireless module. Flash your own firmware, extend with sensors, or use our starter code library.

04
Dust-Sealed Enclosures

IP54-rated electronics bay keeps grit, sand, and light rain out. Designed for the bush, tested in the red dirt.

05
Sensor Expansion Pack

Ultrasonic distance, IR proximity, ambient light, and temperature sensors included. Snap them in with colour-coded JST connectors.

06
Field Repair Manual

A printed, laminated assembly and repair guide. Water-resistant pages, step-by-step diagrams — built to survive wherever you are.

How It Works

From Box to Bush in Four Steps

01
Unbox & Inventory

Every component is bagged, labelled, and numbered. Cross-check against the included parts list — nothing is missing, everything matters.

02
Assemble the Frame

Bolt the chassis panels together using the hex keys provided. Snap-fit joints hold tight without thread-locking compound on first builds.

03
Wire & Flash

Follow the colour-coded wiring diagram, seat the control board, and upload the starter firmware via USB. No soldering required.

04
Calibrate & Deploy

Run the auto-calibration routine from the app or serial monitor. Set your drive mode, and head into the field. It's that simple.
